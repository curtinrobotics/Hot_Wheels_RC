# Controls

Left joystick (left and right): steering left and right
Right trigger (RT, R3): accelerate
Left trigger (LT, L3): reverse

Steering trim
Left bumpers (LB, L1): trim left
Right bumpers (RB, R1): trim right
D-pad up: reset trim
left bumper (LB/L1) + right bumper (RB/R1) + D-pad up: factory reset saved trim

Note: steering trim is saved and restored after reboot.
